NoDash Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Who we are

NoDash is a free, open-source browser extension that automatically removes em-dashes from ChatGPT responses.

Data collection & usage

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
  • All processing (em-dash removal) happens locally in your browser.
  • We don’t use any analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

Permissions

NoDash only injects a small content script into chatgpt.com. It does not request any additional host permissions, “activeTab,” or background network access.
